Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a vital process that helps save millions of lives each year. It is a simple yet powerful act that can make a significant difference in someone's life.

  • A single donation can save up to three lives
  • Someone needs blood every two seconds
  • Only 38% of the population is eligible to donate blood

The Donation Process

1

Registration

Complete a registration form with your personal information and medical history.

2

Screening

A healthcare professional will check your temperature, blood pressure, pulse, and hemoglobin levels.

3

Donation

The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes, during which you'll donate about one pint of blood.
